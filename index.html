Spaghetti Carbonara

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making rich and creamy spaghetti carbonara! This classic Italian dish combines al dente pasta, crispy pancetta, and a silky egg-and-cheese sauce for a comforting meal that's both simple and indulgent. Follow this easy recipe to create a restaurant-quality carbonara right in your own kitchenâ€”perfect for cozy dinners or impressing your guests.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 150g (5 oz) pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 cup (100g) freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese (or Parmesan)
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (just a drizzle, optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Cook the Pancetta/Guanciale
  3. Mix Eggs and Cheese
  4. Combine
  5. Serve
